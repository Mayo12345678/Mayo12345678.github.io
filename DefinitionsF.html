Learn These Words

The definitions of words in any science exam is crucial as without definitions you cannot pass the test. All the words below are buttons, so once you think you have the correct definition, click on the button to see if you are right!

Force

What is a Force?

×
A push or a pull on an object.
Balanced Forces

Balanced Forces

×
When an object has 0 net force acting on it, it's forces are balanced. This means that the object can either be stationary, or travelling at a constant speed.
Net Force

Net Force

×
The vector sum of all opposing forces acting on an object.
Unbalanced Forces

Unbalanced Force

×
When an object has a net force, it has unbalanced forces acting on it. This causes the object to accelerate or decelerate when in motion.
Mass

You are massive

×
The amount of matter in objects. Measured in kilograms (kg).
Weight

Weight due to Gravity

×
The amount of force acting on an object due to gravity. Weight is measured in Newtons.
Acceleration

Getting faster, and faster

×
Measured in (metres per second) per second, it is the ability to gain speed.
Friction

Rub rub rub

×
Acts to oppose motion, friction occurs when two objects rub against each other.
Air Resistance

Drag Force

×
Friction caused by air.
Pressure

Under Pressure

×
A measure of how much force is applied over a certain area. Measured in Pascals or Force per metre squared. more. The formula for calculating pressure is P = F/A
